About Dental Zone

Since 2018, oral healthcare built around trust.

Dental Zone Mianwali is a modern dental clinic committed to delivering high-quality oral healthcare in a comfortable, welcoming environment. It has become one of the trusted dental practices in the Mianwali region, offering comprehensive services for patients of all ages — combining experienced professionals, modern technology, and strict sterilization protocols.

Our Mission

Care that's ethical, affordable, and advanced.

To improve oral health by providing ethical, affordable, and advanced dental care while ensuring every patient feels comfortable, respected, and confident in their smile.

Our Vision

Leading dental care in the region.

To become the leading dental healthcare provider in the region by maintaining the highest standards of clinical excellence, innovation, and patient satisfaction.

Dr. Muhammad Hanif Khan Niazi graduated with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from de'Montmorency College of Dentistry, Lahore, in 2006.
